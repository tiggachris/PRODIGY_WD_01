1. Call of Duty: Warzone Adding Wild New 'Splat' Anti-Cheat Measure
Activision is implementing an interesting anti-cheat feature called Splat to deal with hackers running riot in Call of Duty: Warzone. Splat will turn hackers into walking targets, making them easy to spot and kill. This should make the game more enjoyable for legitimate players.

2. Baldur's Gate 3 Xbox Series S Optimization Will Result in Performance Boost for Everybody
Larian Studios has announced that its upcoming optimization patch for Baldur's Gate 3 on Xbox Series S will also result in performance improvements for Series X, PS5, and PC gamers. The patch will be released on November 15, 2023.

3. Ubisoft Is On The NFT Sauce Again
Ubisoft has announced a new partnership with Web3 platform Immutable to create "a fresh new experience that players will love." This comes after the company's previous NFT initiative, Quartz, was met with widespread criticism from players. It remains to be seen what Ubisoft has planned this time around, but it's clear that the company is still interested in NFTs.
